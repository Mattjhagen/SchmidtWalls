Trusted retaining walls in La Vista, NE

La Vista homeowners rely on retaining walls to level backyards, protect foundations, and create clean planting beds and patios that last for decades.

Schmidt Construction has built retaining walls across the Omaha metro — including La Vista, NE — for nearly 50 years. Every wall we build starts with proper site drainage and engineered base preparation, because a retaining wall is only as good as what's behind it. That's why our walls hold their line through Nebraska's freeze-thaw cycles when cheaper builds bulge, crack, and fail.

Whether you need to level a sloped backyard, protect a foundation, create a walkout, or add terraced planting beds, we'll design a wall that fits your property and budget. Free, no-obligation on-site estimates throughout La Vista.
