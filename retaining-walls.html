Curved timber retaining wall

  • Soil pressure engineering

Our Omaha retaining wall installation process

1

On-Site Assessment

We evaluate slope, drainage, soil structure, elevations, and access.

2

Wall Design & Material Selection

We tailor your wall to match the function, height, landscape, and aesthetic.

3

Excavation & Foundation Prep

We excavate to the correct depth and install a compressed aggregate base for stability.

4

Wall Construction & Reinforcement

Our walls are built using strict engineering principles that prevent shifting and leaning.

5

Drainage System Installation

Proper drainage prevents hydrostatic pressure — the #1 cause of wall failure.

6

Cleanup & Final Inspection

Your property is left clean, safe, and ready to enjoy.
