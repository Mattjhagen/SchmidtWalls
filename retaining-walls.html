Omaha's trusted retaining walls contractor

Schmidt Construction has built retaining walls across the Omaha metro for over 50 years. Every wall starts with proper site evaluation, engineered drainage, and a compacted base — because a retaining wall is only as good as what's behind it. That's why our walls hold their line through Nebraska's brutal freeze-thaw cycles when cheaper builds bulge, crack, and fail.

What we offer

Block Retaining Walls

Engineered segmental block walls — the most popular choice for residential and commercial projects. Proper geogrid reinforcement on taller walls.

Timber Retaining Walls

Natural pressure-treated timber walls ideal for landscaped and garden settings. Cost-effective for shorter walls up to 4 feet.

Commercial & Structural Walls

Large-scale retaining walls for commercial lots, road cuts, and parking structures. Engineered to spec.

Terraced & Garden Walls

Multi-tier walls that turn steep, unusable grades into beautiful, level planting beds and usable yard space.

Drainage Systems

Every wall we build includes proper French drains, weep holes, and gravel backfill to prevent hydrostatic pressure failure.
