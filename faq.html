Retaining Wall & Concrete FAQs

How much does a retaining wall cost in Omaha?

Most residential block retaining walls run roughly $30–$100+ per square face foot installed, depending on height, length, materials, and drainage. We provide free on-site estimates with exact pricing for your specific project.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes — free, no-obligation on-site estimates across the Omaha metro. Call (402) 320-2600 or request one online.

What areas do you serve?

Omaha, Bellevue, Papillion, La Vista, Elkhorn, Gretna, Council Bluffs, and surrounding communities.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — licensed and insured, family-owned since 1973.

What types of retaining walls do you build?

Engineered block walls, timber walls, commercial/structural walls, terraced walls, and garden walls — all with proper drainage.

How long does a retaining wall project take?

Most residential walls take 3–7 days depending on size and site conditions. We'll give you a timeline with your estimate.

Do you handle permits?

Yes — we handle city permits when required (typically for walls over 4 feet in Omaha).
