# Review Request Templates — Schmidt Construction

Reviews are the #1 local SEO ranking factor. Send one of these to happy customers
a day or two after the job is finished.

## Your Google review link
After your Google Business Profile is verified:
1. Go to your Business Profile → **Ask for reviews** → copy the short link
   (looks like `https://g.page/r/XXXXXXXX/review`).
2. Paste it where the templates below say `[GOOGLE REVIEW LINK]`.

## Tips
- Ask in person when you finish the job ("We'd really appreciate a review — I'll text you the link").
- Send within 1-3 days while it's fresh.
- Never offer payment/discounts for reviews (against Google policy).
- Respond to every review (good or bad) — it boosts ranking and shows you care.
- Aim for a steady trickle (2-4/month) rather than a big burst.
